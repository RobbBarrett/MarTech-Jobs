Intent

Buyer behavior reveals intent long before a lead says it out loud

Website behavior, recency, depth of engagement, and account-level activity can signal evaluation patterns much earlier than static form data alone.

Signals that matter

  • Pricing page activity
  • Technical content consumption
  • Repeated visits in short windows
  • Multiple people from the same company engaging

Scoring philosophy

The scoring model should think like a statistician, not a campaign manager.

AI

Practical AI in marketing operations starts with better data, not hype

AI becomes useful when it classifies, normalizes, enriches, and interprets marketing data in ways that make the downstream system smarter.

High-value AI use cases

  • Job title and department normalization
  • Demographic standardization across inconsistent inputs
  • Deduplication and identity cleanup
  • Behavior pattern interpretation for intent scoring

Why it matters

Cleaner inputs create better segmentation, stronger scoring, improved routing, and more trustworthy analytics downstream.

Insight

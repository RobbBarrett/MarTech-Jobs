Intent

Behavior says more than a form field ever will.

Munchkin data captures the digital trail buyers leave behind: repeat visits, recency, depth, content patterns, pricing engagement, documentation interest, and multiple stakeholders from the same account.

Signals worth weighting

  • Pricing and product page activity
  • Technical content and documentation views
  • Short-window return visits
  • Cross-contact engagement from one company

Scoring philosophy

The scoring model should think like a statistician, not a campaign manager. Weight the signals that correlate with movement, not the ones that merely look busy.

Intelligence

AI becomes useful when it improves the data and sharpens the signal.

I am less interested in AI as a gimmick and more interested in it as an operational multiplier. The best uses are pragmatic: data cleansing, standardization, enrichment, behavior analysis, and pattern recognition that would otherwise be costly, brittle, or manual.

Standardize

Use structured API prompts to classify job titles, functions, seniority, and management level.

Enrich

Augment records with signals gathered from disparate systems and public sources.

Deduplicate

Resolve people and account identities more intelligently before the CRM inherits the mess.

Insight

Real ROI analysis happens in analytics systems, not inside Marketo reporting.

Marketo should produce clean, meaningful extracts that can be combined with CRM, ad platform, and sales data. That is where multi-touch attribution, multi-attribute ROI, and channel performance become believable instead of decorative.

I treat AI as a force multiplier for good operations, not a replacement for expertise. In a role like this, that means using AI to make Marketo more reliable, more documented, and easier to work with—while keeping humans firmly in control.

  • Use AI to draft documentation, QA checklists, and playbooks, then refine with human judgment.

AI, operations, and responsible scale
Practical, not hype‑driven

I treat AI as a force multiplier for good operations, not a replacement for expertise. In a role like this, that means using AI to make Marketo more reliable, more documented, and easier to work with—while keeping humans firmly in control.

  • Use AI to draft documentation, QA checklists, and playbooks, then refine with human judgment.
  • Apply AI to pattern‑spotting in logs, sync issues, and performance data—flagging anomalies for review.
  • Support marketers with guided prompts and templates that keep campaigns on‑brand and on‑process.
  • Keep a clear line: AI can suggest, summarize, and surface patterns; humans decide and approve.
Principle: AI augments, humans own decisions.
Outcome: Faster ops, stronger governance.
